Examples of objectiveness

Graphs

Are objective they show data, the causation or correlation may be objective or subjective depending on sample data, and if it was a fair test, but the graph itself is objective.


The Earth is round

This is objective as it is factually correct that the Earth is round. It is subjective if people say otherwise.


Covid-19 is real

Covid-19 is real is true and if others say otherwise that is subjective.
